Detailed Description

Synapse Class

This class represents a single directed synapse connection to neurons of an artificial neural net. The synapse is described by the pre- and postsynaptic neuron and its synaptic weight.

The constructor

You have to give the pre- and postsynaptic neuron here. Those cannot be changed later. Create a new synapse if you want to connect other neurons instead of the present ones.

The constructor also "registers" the new synapse at the two neurons so that the synapse is inserted into their corresponding lists.
